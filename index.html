Frequently Asked Questions

What should I be testing? +

We want an installer and end user perspective on the following critical use cases. Please use the amp in one or more of these scenarios if possible and note issues or suggestions in the beta interface.

  • TV Speaker Amplifier (ARC) - Behind or near TV mounting applications
  • Zone Amplification - Use the amp to amplify a small indoor zone
  • Outdoor Amplification - Connect to your outdoor speakers

Specific areas to test and provide feedback include:

  1. Mounting: What issues or suggestions do you have related to the mounting hardware available to you?
  2. Connection: Note any issues connecting or limitations that make the use scenario you are attempting challenging.
  3. Setup: Utilize the app to configure the settings to optimize your use case. What challenges or difficulties are you having related to setup?
  4. Full app testing: Peruse all the settings in the app and let us know if anything is not working as expected or requires clarity.
  5. User Guide: Was there critical information that you needed that was not intuitive or available to you?
  6. Normal Usage: Listen to music and/or watch content as you would normally. Does the amp satisfy your needs? Are there any limitations? Please note any issues or suggestions.

How do I set up the app? +

For iOS users:

  1. Download and install TestFlight from the App Store.
  2. Once installed, you can access the iOS version of the Sonance app at: https://testflight.apple.com/join/vCtfzYcV

For Android users:

  1. Download and install the APK linked here: Sonance_1.1.7_2024-09-29-11-31.apk

To get started:

Make sure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone. The amp will communicate with the phone via BLE. You do not need to pair with the amplifier. It will be automatically discovered. The amp will show up as discoverable in the app if connected to power and in range of your phone.

Select the amp once it appears in the app home screen, and then you will have access to the range of settings available to test.
