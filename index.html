Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this beta program? +

The SONANCE Beta Program is designed to gather valuable feedback on our products to help us enhance performance, usability, and quality.

How do I submit feedback? +

Click on the "Provide Feedback" button above to send your insights and suggestions directly to our team.

Where can I find the Quick Start Guide? +

You can view the Quick Start Guide by clicking on the "View Quick Start Guide" button above.

What should I be testing? +

We want feedback on the installer and end-user perspective across several critical use cases:

  • TV Speaker Amplifier (ARC) - Testing in behind or near TV mounting applications.
  • Zone Amplification - Using the amp to amplify a small indoor zone.
  • Outdoor Amplification - Connecting to outdoor speakers.

Specific areas to test and provide feedback include mounting, connection, setup, app usage, the user guide, and normal usage scenarios. Please note any issues or suggestions encountered in these areas.

How do I set up the app? +

For iOS users:

  1. Download and install TestFlight from the App Store.
  2. Access the iOS version of the Sonance app at: https://testflight.apple.com/join/vCtfzYcV

For Android users:

  1. Download and install the APK linked here: Sonance_1.1.7_2024-09-29-11-31.apk

Make sure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone. The amp will communicate with the phone via BLE and will appear in the app's home screen if powered on and within range. Select the amp in the app to access available settings for testing.
